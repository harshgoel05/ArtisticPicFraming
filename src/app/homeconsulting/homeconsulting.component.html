Home Consulting
In addition to quality in-house preservation and consulting, we also provide services to travel to your location! Many of our clients may live a substantial distance from our berkeley headquarters or may not have the time to consult with us in our design room, therefore we offer to bring moulding, mat, and glazing samples to your home! We then assess your home, decor, personal aesthetic and taste in order to best suit the needs of your art work.

On location framing design and consulting (including mat, moulding, and glazing samples)
Customizing an art program for each home project
On location installation
Transportation and delivery
