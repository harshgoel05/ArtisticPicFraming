Design and Process

BRINGING US YOUR ART

We are able to work with any piece of art you may have collected from special trips around the world, museum galleries, or the simple but special memorabilia that you have kept around the house. Along with prints, photos, or fabrics we can also handle all your stretches and 3-dimensional objects you value.

ASSESSING YOUR AESTHETIC

Through this process we can get to know a little more about you. We can hone in on where your style and taste may lie and find out whether you enjoy the stylish sense of modernity or have an ornate/rustic sort of taste.. or possibly somewhere in-between. This process allows you to be hands on with the direction of your piece and to fully customize your treasure.

CHOOSING MATERIALS

We hold in our stock hundreds and hundreds of wood and metal moulding options, ready to cut and join in-house! We can browse through handcrafted natural woods or a glossy and metallic look, its up to you! Once we get going we can then expand our tool kit to choose mat colors and textures or embellishments like fillets, double mats, raised floats, and shadow boxes.

CREATING A BUDGET AND PLAN OF ACTION

Once the materials are chosen we asses the budget and timeline that works for your calendar wallet.
We then lay out the timeline of work and completion. We will also provide delivery and installation for an additional fee.
